Dominoes Game Score

Dominoes Game Score is a scorecard for Dominoes fans that want to get rid of the pencil and the paper. Keep the score of the game in this intuitive app and focus on play the game and defeat at your opponent.

No more pencil and paper. Dominoes Game Score provides a simple scorecard for your dominoes games. Whether you are playing with teams or free for all, you can customize your Dominoes Game Score to specify teams or players names and the game score. You play for 100? 150?

Dominoes Game Score allows you to configure the game score as you want. Keep track of who is winning games using your Dominoes Game Score.
